Why run locally?
Browsers block websites from reaching devices on your home network — that's why connecting from printara.netlify.app doesn't work without changing settings. Running the queue on your own computer bypasses this completely. It opens in your browser just like a website, but talks directly to your printer.

Features
Everything you need to run a print farm
🔌

Universal USB Connection

Auto-detects any serial port. Works with Marlin, Klipper (USB serial), and most 3D printer firmware out of the box.

📋

Drag & Drop Queue

Drop .gcode files onto the app to queue them instantly. Reorder, remove, or add files at any time — even mid-print.

⇥

Auto-Eject After Print

Configurable G-code sequence runs after every print — parks the head, moves the bed forward, and cools down automatically.

🌡

Live Temperature Display

Hotend and bed temperatures update in real time. No more wondering if your printer is at temp.

📟

Built-in G-code Console

Type any G-code command directly into the console for manual control. Full response log included.

⏸

Pause, Resume & Cancel

Full print control at your fingertips. Pause to check a layer, resume when ready, or cancel and move to the next job.

How It Works
Set it and forget it
1

Connect your printer

Plug in via USB, select the port from the dropdown, choose your baud rate (usually 115200), and hit Connect.

2

Add files to the queue

Click "Add Files" or drag and drop your sliced .gcode files. Reorder them however you like.

3

Hit Start Queue

Printara sends G-code line by line, tracks progress, and shows live temperature and console output.

4

Auto-eject & next job

When a print finishes, Printara runs your eject sequence to present the bed, then automatically starts the next item in the queue.
